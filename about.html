Sama Srinivas

It's nice to meet you!

I am an aspiring designer whose introduction to the arts was through self-taught painting. I ultimately made the switch to digital art and design in university.

I am an undergraduate student at New York University studying Interactive Media Arts at the Tisch School of the Arts.

To Me Design Is...

I am attracted to a simple and beautiful design aesthetic, a practice I try to incorporate in my projects as well as in my work as a student graphic designer for NYU Athletics.

Industries that pique my interest include visual design, UI/UX design, web design, graphic design, marketing, and communications.
